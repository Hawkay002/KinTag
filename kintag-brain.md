## What is it & How It Works
KinTag is a digital safety net for families. 
1. Create a Profile: Build a digital ID with emergency contacts, medical info, and behavioral details.
2. Attach the Tag: Print the QR code or program an NFC sticker and attach it to a backpack, collar, or medical bracelet.
3. Stay Protected: If they wander off, a Good Samaritan scans the tag, and the owner instantly receives their GPS location.

## Who is it for?
- Kids & Children: Precise GPS location and immediate community alerts.
- Pets & Animals: Displays vaccinations, microchip IDs, and vet contacts. Better than engraved tags.
- Seniors & Medical: Details medical conditions and primary caregivers for elderly prone to wandering.

## Core Features
- No App Required: Finders just use their standard smartphone camera.
- Precision GPS: Finders can securely share exact coordinates with one tap.
- Passive Scan (IP Fallback): Even if the finder denies GPS, KinTag grabs their general IP-based city/region.
- Lost Mode (Panic Button): Transforms the ID into a high-alert distress signal with a flashing missing banner and pulsing emergency dialer.
- KinAlert Broadcasts: Sends localized push notifications to all other KinTag users in the same Zip Code to help search.
- Co-Guardian Family Sharing: Invite up to 5 family members to manage profiles and receive alerts simultaneously.
- Caretaker Mode: Generate secure, self-destructing access links for babysitters or dog walkers.
- Secure Document Vault: Upload sensitive records (Rabies certs, Autism IDs). They stay blurred and locked until the finder shares GPS or calls.
- Google Wallet Integration: Save mobile IDs as a pass in Google Wallet.
- Privacy / Anti-Download: Right-clicking and long-press saving are disabled to protect photos and data.
- Instant Kill Switch: Remotely disable a profile instantly if a tag is lost or stolen.
- Danger Zone: Users can permanently delete their account and all data at any time.

## FAQs & Common Questions
- Cost? Absolutely free forever. No monthly subscriptions.
- Does it have a battery? No, it relies on the finder's phone battery and cellular data.
- Do I have to buy tags from KinTag? No. You can download and print QR codes at home or buy cheap blank NFC tags from Amazon and program them yourself using free apps.
- Will the QR code expire? Never, as long as the account is active.
- Can I create profiles for multiple pets/kids? Yes, from a single dashboard.
- What if I move or change numbers? Just update the cloud dashboard; the physical tag updates instantly.
